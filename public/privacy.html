Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2025

1. Overview

ONE EIGHT ("we", "our") operates the web-based game service at oneeightgame.com. This policy describes what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

2. Data We Collect

Account data: When you create an account, we collect your email address and the display name you choose. Authentication is handled via Supabase Auth.

Gameplay data: We record game results, move histories, ratings, and statistics associated with your account. This data is used to power leaderboards, match history, and analysis features.

Payment data: If you subscribe to a paid plan, payment is processed by Paddle. We do not store your full card number or billing details. Paddle provides us with basic transaction records (subscription status, billing date, amount).

Usage data: We may collect basic technical information such as browser type, device type, and approximate region for operational and security purposes. We do not use advertising trackers.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and operate the game service
  • To authenticate your account and maintain session security
  • To process subscription payments and manage billing status
  • To display game history, ratings, and competitive standings
  • To communicate service-related updates (account or billing notices)
  • To investigate abuse, cheating, or violations of our Terms of Service

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

Supabase: We use Supabase for database storage and authentication. Data is stored on Supabase-managed infrastructure.

Paddle: We use Paddle as our payment processor and merchant of record. Paddle's privacy policy applies to data collected during payment.

Google Fonts: Fonts are loaded from Google Fonts, which may involve data transfer to Google's servers.

5. Data Retention

We retain account and gameplay data for as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, we will remove your personal data within a reasonable period, subject to any legal obligations.

6. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us at contact@oneeightgame.com. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

7. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, please contact us at contact@oneeightgame.com.
