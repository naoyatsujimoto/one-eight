Refund Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2025

Subscriptions

ONE EIGHT memberships are billed on a monthly basis. You may cancel at any time, and your access will continue until the end of the current billing period. We do not issue prorated refunds for the remaining days of a cancelled subscription.

If you believe you were charged in error — for example, due to a technical issue or an unintended renewal — please contact us within 14 days of the charge. We will review your request and issue a refund if appropriate.

Competitions and Events

Entry fees for official competitions or events are generally non-refundable once the event has begun. If an event is cancelled by us, affected participants will receive a full refund of their entry fee.

Specific refund terms for individual events will be stated at the time of entry.

No Physical Products

ONE EIGHT is a digital service. There are no physical goods associated with this service, so no shipping or physical return policy applies.

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund or report a billing issue, please contact our support team with your account email and a brief description of the issue.

Email: contact@oneeightgame.com
We aim to respond within 2 business days.
